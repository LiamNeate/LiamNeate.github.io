Devsoc


Devsoc (which stand for Developers Scoiety) is a society at University that is mainly about coding. It is a very active society with loads of activites happening most of the time, as well as fun challenges they have. Because of Covid, I have not had a chance to go to an in person meeting, but I was able to participate in their Crypto Challenge!

For the Crypto Challenge, the Devsoc homepage had been hacked and I had to find the key to get the website back (Hackers logo shown on middle picture). It started simply by looking through the source code of the website to find clues, which told you to go to their instagram page. From there, there was a secret message in one of their posts, which turned out to be a Ceasar cipher. Once that was decryted, it gave a very vague hint saying 'An alliance between three, forged in secret in the heart of an empire, lost but not forgotten this was the first of many, the beginning of a new age uncover their names to find your next stage'. After some research, I found this was refering to the First Triumvate, which didn't really give me much to go on, so I decide to examine the post further. I noticed it had a hashtag in it, which was also used on a similar page called 'incognito_caesar' and since Caeser was part of the First Triumvate, I kne this was the right path. On his page, was another clue whih told me to go and check their github, which was linked in their instagram bio. Once there, I could see that there was a file which this account had tried to hide a code in an image. I downloaded the image, and looked through the source code, where I found password 1 of 3.

Next, I went back to the original post that got me to that instagram to see if there was any more clues. I found there was another page that had that hashtag, called 'pompeyrulz' which was obviously related. There was another code in one of their posts, along with a reference to a video, where they talked about a certain type of cipher. Using that cipher, I decoded the message to find password 2 of 3

For the final pass, I looked through the rest of their posts, and found that they had posted about a twitter account called 'CrassusTheBot', and since Crassus was part of the First Triumvate, I checkd it out. Once there, I found out that this was a bot that responded to specific tweet hashtags. After a lot of trial and error, it finally gave me a code, which was coded by a fernet cipher (I found this out as they referenced fernet a lot in their posts). Once I combined the code they had given me, with the code they had in the header, I finally got password 3 of 3, and submitted my result.
