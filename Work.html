Part-Time Work


As a part-time job, I am currently working at Morrisons in Bulwell, where I have the job of packing and picking people's orders, who order from Amazon. I started this job on the 21st of November, and on my first day in, I had my induction. During my nduction we went through all the usual health and saftey rules, as well as what we have to do. After the presentation, we set up an online account where we could view our hours and any other information we needed. Once done, we had a quick tour of the warehouse and saw current pick and packers working in a relativly small area. We then where assigned two traning days where we would have to come in and shadow a current pick and packer. You can find the Morrisons website here
