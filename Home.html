About Me

Hi, my name is Liam Neate and I am currently a student at Nottingham Trent University, where, I am studying Software Engineering. Before that, I was a student at Highcliffe Sixth form, back in my hometown. I was always very passionate about computers and programming, but it wasn't until going to Highcliffe Sixth form that I decided I wanted to study it full time.

In my spare time, I mainly enjoy playing videogames with my friends. I find this a really nice way to relax, as well as a good motivator for my work, as my goal in life is to develop video games. I also like to use this time to talk to my friends about the work they are doing, and discussing relevant news that has happened.

Work

Before coming to University, I had a job at Curry's PC World in my hometown. I was very passionate about my job and alwasy enjoyed going in. My job there consisted of talking to customers about our products as well as selling to them. It also meant that I had to keep on my toes and stay informed about the latest tech. This further fueled my pasion for technology and how the future will be shapped around them.

My first job, however, was at Hoburne Retail Park which was a big holiday park in my hometown. There I worked in the arcade, fixing arcade machines, filling up tickets, giving out prizes, cleaning up at the end of the day, etc. From time to time, I also had to help cleaning tables when it was busy. Normally, I would be the only one looking after the arcade during my shift, so it was very lonely and stressful, which eventually caused me to leave and work at PC World.
